# Data Dictionary

## hourly_load_and_renewables.csv

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| hour | Hour of synthetic daily profile |
| demand_mwh | Electricity demand in MWh |
| solar_mwh | Solar generation in MWh |
| wind_mwh | Wind generation in MWh |
| temperature_c | Outdoor temperature in Celsius |

## generator_fleet.csv

Synthetic generator fleet with nameplate capacity, variable cost, emissions intensity, forced outage rate, and capacity credit.

## storage_assumptions.csv

Teaching assumptions for battery, pumped hydro, and thermal storage assets.

## household_energy_burden.csv

Synthetic household affordability dataset. Energy burden is annual energy cost divided by annual income.

## climate_stressors.csv

Synthetic asset-level climate-risk stressor table for infrastructure resilience examples.

All datasets are synthetic teaching data unless otherwise documented.
